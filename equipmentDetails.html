TREADMILL

Overview

The treadmill is a versatile cardio machine used for full body conditioning and cardiovascular health improvement. Perfect for both beginners and advanced users, it offers customizable workouts through speed and incline adjustments.

Key Features

Speed Range

1-12 mph

Incline

0-15%

Display

LCD Screen

Correct Usage & Safety

  • Stay in the middle of the belt while running
  • Maintain proper distance from handlebars
  • Avoid moving too far back on the treadmill
  • Always attach the safety chord to your clothing
  • Start slow and gradually increase speed
  • Use the handrails when adjusting speed or incline
