Bench Press

The bench press is a compound exercise that targets your chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Difficulty

Intermediate

Target Muscles

Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

Equipment

Barbell, Bench

Instructions

  1. Lie on the bench with your feet flat on the ground
  2. Grip the barbell slightly wider than shoulder width
  3. Lower the bar to your chest with control
  4. Press the bar back up to the starting position
  5. Repeat for desired reps
